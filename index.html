Introduction of MLA

Medicos Learning Association is a non-profit welfare concept. It has been initiated withthe idea of providing a platform for the Passionate Personals who want to bring a positivechange in Healthcare sector of Pakistan. MLCA aims to promote all the Medical Fields in ourcountry. Our services are centered around promotion of hard work, embellishment of the social,professional & academic skills and giving vast experiences to our volunteers.Whether you are senior or junior, experienced or newbie; If you have dedication,commitment to your goals, and want to do something unique and big in your life.
